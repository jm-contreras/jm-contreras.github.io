About

I co-founded and serve as CEO of Aymara, an AI safety evaluation company that grew to serve Fortune 50 enterprise customers and leading AI labs—without any funding. Before that, I led legal applied science at Uber, where my team's work was cited in court decisions and influenced billion-dollar regulatory outcomes.

I have a PhD in cognitive neuroscience from Harvard and an AB from Princeton.

Born in New York City and raised in Bolivia and the U.S., I run marathons, practice yoga, and am starting a band with AI musicians.
