I am a principal data scientist at Capital Onezz.

Previously, I served as a behavioral scientist at the White House Social and Behavioral Sciences Team, the senior data scientist of the Democratic National Committee, and a decision scientist at The Walt Disney Company.

Before my professional career, I was a doctoral student conducting neuroimaging experiments to understand how people think about social groups. I am formally trained as an experimental psychologist in social neuroscience, with an AB in psychology from Princeton University and an AM and a PhD in psychology from Harvard University.
