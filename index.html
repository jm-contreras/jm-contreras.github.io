About

In June, I am joining Capital One as a principal data scientist.

Previously, I served as a behavioral scientist at the White House Social and Behavioral Sciences Team, a data scientist at the Democratic National Committee, and a decision scientist at The Walt Disney Company.

Before my professional career, I used neuroimaging to understand how people think about social groups (see Publications). I was formally trained as an experimental psychologist at Princeton University and Harvard University.

In my spare time, I help to lead DataKind DC and program a Monopoly simulator. I used to write about data science and neuroscience.
